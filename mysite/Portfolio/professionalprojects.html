Pillow Talk

Pillow Talk is a PHP ecommerce site that I designed and coded for a client who makes handmade pillows. We used Paypal integration for its ease of use and its familiarity among online shoppers. We plan to expand the order options in the future to allow customers to select custom pillow colors and designs and upload their own images for personalized products.
